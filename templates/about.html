About LocateUs

When you lose a child, the first thing to do is to report to the nearest police station. However, report them to us, as we maintain a real-time database of lost and found children. All you have to do is report here and leave the rest to us. We re-unite parents or guardians with their children



Benefits of our system


Reuniting families

LocateUs increases the likelihood of reuniting lost children with their parents or guardians with the minimum of inconvenience.

Reporting made easier

Making the reporting process for the public and Police more efficient.

Hopes uplifted

Found children becoming more easily identifiable and returned to people.
